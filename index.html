Experiences

Citadel Securities (Miami, FL), Software Engineering Intern, summer 2023

As part of the message bus team, contributed to a reliable, highly efficient, and customizable distributed publisher-subscriber system to support trading and research across the firm.

Enabled the monitoring of publisher and subscriber liveness in the distributed message bus. Implemented a robust retry system that recovers from interruption of the gRPC heartbeats. Supports indirect maintenance of heartbeats via brokers to reduce load on the discovery backend.

Captured and exposed the connectivity status between publishers and subscribers to reduce time needed to diagnose issues from 30+ minutes (of manually examining multiple logs) to seconds. Reports detailed, per-shard connection state between every pair of publisher and subscriber and records the latest state change event across multicast and TCP channels. Built a tool to construct connection graphs to visualize the system state. Designed a flexible data model for connectivity that supports different types of underlying transports.

Spotnana Technology (Palo Alto, CA), Software Engineering Intern, summer 2022

Demonstrated the integration live to United Airlines representatives, passed their tests, and received their official approval to deploy to travelers at 50+ corporate clients. Pushed the project into production.

Designed a cache to reduce the loading time of the trip details page, achieving a 3x speedup on a cache hit. The cache saves recent NDC requests and responses to avoid sending unnecessary network calls that fetch information about the same trip multiple times.

Created a system to ensure itinerary data displayed in the app always stays up to date by detecting and reconciling changes to the booking made outside the app (such as flight delays and voluntary changes made by the passenger through outside channels). Deployed the system into production for American Airlines NDC travelers.
